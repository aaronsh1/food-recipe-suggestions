American-style mango pancakes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 6-12

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 110g/4oz self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • pinch salt
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 150ml/¼ pint milk
  • 4 mango slices, chopped
  • dash vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • honey, to serve

Method

  1. Place the flour, baking powder and salt into a large bowl. Make a well in the middle of the flour mixture, add the eggs and whisk to combine. Gradually add the milk and whisk to make a thick batter. Stir in the chopped mango and leave to rest for five minutes.

  2. Heat the oil and butter in a frying pan and place spoonfuls of the batter into the pan. Fry for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden-brown. Remove from the pan and repeat with the remaining batter.

  3. To serve, place the pancakes onto a serving plate and drizzle with honey.
