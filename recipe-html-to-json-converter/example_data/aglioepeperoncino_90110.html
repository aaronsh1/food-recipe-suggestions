Aglio e peperoncino

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

By Aldo Zilli
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 red chilli, chopped
  • 200g/7oz dried linguine, cooked according to packet instructions
  • 6 fresh basil leaves, torn

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and fry the garlic and chilli for 1-2 minutes, or until softened. Add the cooked linguine to the pan and toss to coat, then stir in the basil leaves.

  2. To serve, place the linguine onto a serving plate.
