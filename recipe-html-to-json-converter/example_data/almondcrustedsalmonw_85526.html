Almond-crusted salmon with chilli spinach

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

2 people

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the almond-crusted salmon

  • 2 tbsp whole almonds
  • large handful fresh parsley
  • 50g/1¾oz breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic and horseradish mustard
  • 150g/5½oz salmon fillet, skinned

For the chilli spinach

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 150g/5½oz spinach
  • pinch chilli flakes
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. To make the salmon crust, place the almonds, parsley, breadcrumbs, a dash of olive oil and seasoning into a blender and pulse to combine.

  2. Spread the garlic and horseradish mustard over the salmon fillet and then roll in the breadcrumbs. Pat to ensure the crumbs stick to the salmon.

  3. Heat the remaining olive oil in a frying pan, add the salmon and pan fry for 3-4 minutes each side.

  4. For the chilli spinach, heat the olive oil in a wok, add the garlic and cook gently for 1-2 minutes. Add the spinach, chilli and seasoning and toss to slightly wilt the spinach. Leave to cool slightly and then squeeze to remove the excess moisture from the spinach

  5. To serve, spoon into a chef's ring, remove the ring and top with the salmon.
