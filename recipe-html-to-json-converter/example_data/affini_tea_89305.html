Affini tea

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Try infusing your cocktails with tea for a sophisticated tipple.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 50ml/2fl oz Scotch whisky
  • 1 Lady Grey tea bag
  • 1 tsp peach syrup
  • 2 dashes angostura bitters
  • twist lemon peel, to garnish

Method

  1. Pour the Scotch into a mixing jug with a handful of ice. Dip the teabag in for about 20 seconds to infuse, remove and add the remaining ingredients.

  2. Stir until well combined, then strain the cocktail into a tumbler filled with ice. Garnish with the lemon twist.
