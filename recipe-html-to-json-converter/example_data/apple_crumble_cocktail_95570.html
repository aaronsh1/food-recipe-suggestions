Apple crumble cocktail

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

This autumnal cocktail is an apple crumble in a glass - fruity, spicy and warming.

By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 35ml/1¼fl oz whisky
  • 1 tbsp apple liqueur
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • pinch mixed spice
  • 1 tbsp Bramley apple sauce
  • dash sugar syrup

To garnish

  • 1 tsp Bramely apple sauce
  • 1 digestive biscuit, crushed

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake hard.

  2. Strain the cocktail into a tumbler filled with crushed ice and garnish with a teaspoon of apple sauce and a sprinkling of the biscuit.
